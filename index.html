NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME

Maturi Venkata Subba Rao Engineering College

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government-sponsored program in India that aims to instill a sense of social responsibility and commitment in students. At Maturi Venkata Subba Rao Engineering College, we are proud to participate in this program and encourage our students to get involved.

Through the NSS, our students have the opportunity to engage in various community service activities, such as blood donation camps, tree plantation drives, and awareness campaigns on important social issues. These activities not only help the community, but also provide our students with valuable life skills and experiences.
